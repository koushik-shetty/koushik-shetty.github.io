Observability That Actually Works

Reticle transforms the chaotic world of application monitoring into a clear, actionable view of your system's health. We believe observability should help you understand your applications, not overwhelm you with data.

Why Reticle?

Traditional monitoring tools dump endless metrics and logs without context. Reticle intelligently connects the dots, showing you exactly what's happening in your applications and why it matters for your users.

Core Features

  • Unified Data Collection: Logs, metrics, traces, and events in one platform
  • Intelligent Correlation: Automatic linking of related events across your stack
  • Smart Alerting: Context-aware alerts that reduce false positives by 90%
  • Visual Service Maps: Real-time topology visualization of your microservices
  • Custom Dashboards: Drag-and-drop dashboard builder with pre-built templates
  • Anomaly Detection: Machine learning-powered detection of unusual patterns
  • Root Cause Analysis: Automated investigation workflows to speed up troubleshooting

How It Works

Reticle uses advanced correlation algorithms and machine learning to automatically understand the relationships between different parts of your system. Instead of drowning you in data, we surface the insights that matter most.

Our lightweight agents and SDKs integrate with your existing infrastructure in minutes, not days. Whether you're running containers, serverless functions, or traditional VMs, Reticle adapts to your environment.

Integration Ecosystem

  • Cloud Platforms: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, and hybrid environments
  • Container Orchestration: Kubernetes, Docker Swarm, Amazon ECS
  • Programming Languages: Java, Python, Node.js, Go, .NET, Ruby, PHP
  • Databases: PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, Elasticsearch
  • Message Queues: Kafka, RabbitMQ, Amazon SQS, Google Pub/Sub
  • Web Servers: Nginx, Apache, Envoy, HAProxy

Benefits for Your Team

For Developers: Faster debugging with precise error tracking and performance profiling. Understand exactly how your code behaves in production.

For DevOps: Comprehensive infrastructure monitoring with predictive scaling recommendations. Automate incident response with intelligent runbooks.

For Product Teams: Real user monitoring and business metrics correlation. Understand how technical performance impacts user experience and business outcomes.
