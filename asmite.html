Revolutionary Privacy Technology

Asmité represents a new paradigm in digital privacy and identity management. In an era where personal data is constantly harvested and monetized, we're building a solution that puts you back in control.

Why Asmité?

Traditional online services collect and store your personal information on their servers, often without your full knowledge or consent. Asmité flips this model entirely – your data stays with you, in storage locations you control.

Key Features

  • Custom Storage Locations: Store your data anywhere you choose – your own server, cloud storage, or local device
  • Alias System: Create and manage multiple digital identities for different purposes
  • End-to-End Encryption: Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device
  • Zero Knowledge Architecture: We never have access to your unencrypted data
  • Interoperability: Works with existing web services through secure proxy connections

How It Works

Asmité acts as a secure bridge between you and the web. When you interact with online services, Asmité uses your chosen alias and retrieves only the necessary information from your secure storage, keeping your real identity and personal data protected.

The system is designed to be transparent to existing web services while providing you with unprecedented control over your digital footprint.
